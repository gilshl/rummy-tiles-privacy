Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

Rummy Tiles is developed by Black Cat. This privacy policy explains how Rummy Tiles handles user data.

Data We Collect and Use

The app may collect and use the following types of data:

  • Player name, avatar, and user identifiers for login, profile display, online multiplayer, and game functionality.
  • Purchase information related to in-app purchases, used to grant virtual coins and prevent fraud.
  • Advertising identifiers and device identifiers used by advertising SDKs to show ads and measure ad performance.
  • Gameplay and app activity data used for online multiplayer, matchmaking, stability, and improving the game.
  • Diagnostic data such as crashes and performance information, used to fix bugs and improve stability.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as Google Play Services, Google Play Billing, Unity Services, and advertising services. These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

Virtual Coins

Rummy Tiles uses virtual coins only for in-game play. Coins have no real-world cash value, cannot be withdrawn, and cannot be transferred outside the game.

Ads and In-App Purchases

The app may show ads and may offer optional in-app purchases. Rewarded ads may grant virtual coins only inside the game.

Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect data and rely on secure services where supported.

Children

The app is not intended for children under the minimum age required in the user's region.
