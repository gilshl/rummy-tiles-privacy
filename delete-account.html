Delete Account and Data

App: Rummy Tiles

Developer: Black Cat

Last updated: May 6, 2026

You can request deletion of your Rummy Tiles account and associated game data.

How to request deletion

Send an email to:
gili.shalem.support@gmail.com

Please include:

  • The email address or Google account used with the game.
  • Your player name in the game, if available.
  • A clear request such as: "Please delete my Rummy Tiles account and data."

What data will be deleted

  • Player profile data, such as player name and avatar reference.
  • Game progress and virtual coin balance stored by the game.
  • Online multiplayer profile data associated with your game account.

What data may be retained

Some data may be retained where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, payment records, or platform requirements.

Processing time

We will process valid deletion requests within a reasonable period of time.

Virtual coins

Rummy Tiles uses virtual coins only for in-game play. Coins have no real-world cash value, cannot be withdrawn, and cannot be transferred outside the game.
